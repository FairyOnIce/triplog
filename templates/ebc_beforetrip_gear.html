Gears that we bring

Backpack

  1. Osprey Aura AG 50 Womens Hiking Backpack Small Challenger Blue
    • I usually bring this backpack for 2-3 day camping. I usually squeeze in tents and bear canisters into this bag (together with my sleeping bag). But this Nepal trekking does not need such things. So I orinigally thought that 50L backpack may be too large, which turned out to be not true because of the large amount of clothes that we need to bring.
  2. Osprey UltraLight Raincover, Shadow Grey, X-Large
    • Weather in high altitude changes very quickly. Weather forecast says that it may snow in EBC during our week of visit. So I am bringing rain gears.


Clothes



Clothes are the most life-related items that we need to bring. As we will be as low as 1302m altitude in Kathmandu. and as high as almost 5300m altitude in EBC, we need to bring wide rante of cloths that can keep us alive. According to the weather forecast, the temperature in Kathmandu in the week of April 15, 2018 ranges between 13C to almost 30C while the temperature in -16C degree to -7C. So the task is to bring cloths for all seasons and squeeze them into my 50L backpack!
